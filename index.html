03 · FAQ

Asked often.

Do I need CAD experience? Q.01

No. Everything is sliders and one draggable profile curve. If you've used a slicer before, you'll be comfortable in minutes — knowing your printer's quirks helps more than any CAD skill.

Which printers are supported? Q.02

Bambu Lab P1S and X1C are first-class, with the real machine start/end blocks and progress display built in. Presets also cover the A1, A1 mini and Prusa MK4, and the start/end gcode is fully editable so any FDM printer can be dialed in.

Why gcode instead of STL? Q.03

Because the pattern is the toolpath. A coil wall or a stitch texture isn't a solid you can mesh and re-slice — the moment a slicer plans it, the flow, seams and layer rhythm are gone. Writing gcode directly is the whole point.

Will every design actually print? Q.04

The parameters are guard-railed so the file is always mechanically valid — but extreme art still pushes physics. Big overhangs and airy weaves reward starting from the defaults and changing one thing at a time.

Can customers order without getting the file? Q.05

Yes. The Customer Link opens the live 3D design with an order form and no download buttons. The customer plays with the shape; the print file stays with With Layers.

What does it cost? Q.06

The studio is free to use in your browser. With Layers earns by printing the pieces — order through the app, or download the gcode and run it on your own machine.
